Urban Bee Lab

Urban Bee Lab is our affiliated 501(c)(3) non-profit. UBL analyzes beehive data and reports results to elucidate trends and advance our understanding of global pollinator health.

UBL improves honey bee health and extends those approaches to other, lesser studied species of pollinators. Together we aim to educate, inspire and build community through research, programs, and partnerships.

Beekeeping Services

We offer residential and corporate beekeeping services - we install and manage honey beehives at home gardens, business rooftops, and institutional campuses across the US. Our clients keep the honey and participate in our research.
